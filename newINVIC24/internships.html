About the work from home job/internship


Selected intern's day-to-day responsibilities include


  1. Task 1: Create at least 2 engaging posters using Canva, Figma, etc., for Subhansh Sewa Trust or fundraising.
  2. Task 2: Immerse yourself in the Fundraising Department and learn the art of securing financial resources for social initiatives. (This week is specially given to complete the fundraising task with a minimum target of Rs 1000).

  4. 4th Week: Learn the power of communication by preparing a one-minute sharing of your experience about the topic given by your team leader in the weekly meeting or live workshop session by an industry professional.
