WHY TO HANDLE TOXIC WASTE

Mercury

HEALTH HAZARDS

  • Inhalation of mercury vapors can lead to symptoms such as headaches, nausea, respiratory issues, and, in severe cases, neurological and kidney damage.
  • Mercury compounds, such as mercuric oxide used in older laptop batteries, can be hazardous if they come into direct contact with the skin or are ingested.

ENVIRONMENT HAZARDS

  • Landfill sites are contaminated is laptops are not disposed correctly.
  • Mercury is highly persistent in the environment and can remain a hazard for an extended period, especially in aquatic sediments.
  • Causes Soil and Water Contamination.
